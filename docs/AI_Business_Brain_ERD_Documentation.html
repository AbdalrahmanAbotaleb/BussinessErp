System Relational Model

Entities are interconnected through standardized Primary and Foreign Key relationships. The Products table acts as the central hub, linking both revenue (Sales) and capital expenditure (Purchases).

Operational Logic

Atomic Integrity: All Sales and Purchases are wrapped in T-SQL Transactions. This ensures that the Inventory (Products) count is decremented or incremented only when the parent record is successfully committed.

Cascading Deletes: Relation between Headers (Sales/Purchases) and Line Items (SaleItems/PurchaseItems) is maintained via ON DELETE CASCADE, ensuring no orphaned data remains in the system.
